🍄 Mushroom Classification Analysis

Project overview:
Developed a machine learning pipeline to classify mushrooms as edible or poisonous based on physical traits. This project demonstrates the ability to handle categorical data and build high-accuracy classification models for safety-critical decision-making

Tools Used:
Python (Pandas, Scikit-Learn), Seaborn, Matplotlib.

Impact:
Achieved over 95% accuracy in classifying mushrooms, providing a valuable tool for foragers and researchers to identify potentially dangerous species based on easily observable characteristics.

❤️ Heart Disease Diagnostic Study

Project Overview:
Analyzed the UCI Heart Disease dataset to identify the most critical risk factors contributing to cardiovascular issues. I focused on exploratory data analysis (EDA) to find correlations between health metrics like cholesterol levels, age, and heart disease presence.

Tools Used: Python, NumPy, SciPy (Statistical Testing).

Impact:
Identified that "Thalassemia" and "Number of Major Vessels" were the strongest predictors of heart disease, providing a data-driven basis for prioritizing patient screenings.

🌏 Gapminder Global Trends Analysis

Project Overview:
A longitudinal study of global health and wealth metrics across several decades. I visualized the relationship between GDP per capita and life expectancy to track how different continents have progressed over time

Tools Used: Python, Plotly (Interactive Charts), Pandas.

Impact:
Transformed static historical data into dynamic visualizations that clearly illustrate the "Great Divergence" and the subsequent global rise in life expectancy despite economic fluctuations.
